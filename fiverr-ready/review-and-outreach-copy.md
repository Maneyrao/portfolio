# Review and Outreach Copy

## Message to request context from a past client

Hola, como estoy ordenando mi perfil profesional, queria pedirte una breve reseña sobre el trabajo que hicimos.

La idea es contar:

- Que problema habia antes.
- Que construimos o mejoramos.
- Como fue el proceso de trabajo.
- Que cambio despues del proyecto.

No tiene que ser largo ni perfecto. Con 4 o 5 lineas honestas alcanza. Si queres, tambien podes sumar una captura o imagen del proyecto.

Gracias de verdad.

## Fiverr-safe buyer reply when someone asks for external contact

Thanks for your message. To keep everything safe and organized, I prefer to discuss the project here on Fiverr. You can send me the details, references and goals in this chat, and I will guide you through the next steps.

## First response template for new Fiverr lead

Hi, thanks for reaching out.

Before suggesting a solution, I would like to understand the project a bit better:

1. What kind of business or project is it?
2. What do you need to build or improve?
3. Do you already have a website, brand assets or references?
4. What is the main action you want users to take?
5. Do you have a deadline or preferred launch date?

Once I have that context, I can recommend the best scope and package for your case.

## Discovery questions for custom offers

- What is the business goal behind this project?
- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Who will use the website or system?
- What do users need to do inside it?
- What content, products or data do you already have?
- Do you need only frontend or also database/backend?
- Do you need integrations?
- What does success look like after launch?
